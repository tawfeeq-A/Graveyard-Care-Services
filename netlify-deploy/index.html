Our services

What we do.

Every job starts with a WhatsApp message. We confirm details by call before any work begins.

🌿

Deweeding

We clear weeds, grass, and unwanted growth from around the grave. The area is left clean and fully visible.

🪓

Bush removal

We remove bushes and shrubs that cover or damage the grave. Overgrowth is cut back fully.

📐

Grave alignment

We correct graves that have shifted or sit uneven with the floor. The result is a straight, properly aligned grave.

🧱

Floor leveling

We level uneven grave surfaces. Work is confirmed after we assess the site condition with you.

💧

DPC work

Send us photos of the grave. We assess what DPC or protective work is needed, then arrange a call to confirm.

🤝

Custom requests

Send us your request on WhatsApp. We discuss what is needed and plan the work with you directly.
